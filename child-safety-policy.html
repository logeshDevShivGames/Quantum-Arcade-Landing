Child Safety & Protection Policy

Amigly Mobile Application

AMIGLY is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We strictly prohibit child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and take strong measures to prevent, detect, and report such activities.

Our Standards

AMIGLY operates under a zero-tolerance policy regarding any form of child exploitation or abuse. We are dedicated to creating and maintaining a safe platform for all users.

  • AMIGLY does not allow content that exploits, abuses, or endangers children.
  • Accounts found violating child safety policies are immediately suspended or permanently banned.
  • We actively monitor and investigate reported content and user behavior.
  • All user-generated content is subject to automated and manual review processes.
  • We employ industry-standard safety measures to protect minors using our platform.

Important Notice: Any attempt to use AMIGLY for illegal activities involving minors will result in immediate account termination and reporting to law enforcement authorities.

Reporting & Moderation

We take every report seriously and have established clear procedures to address child safety concerns quickly and effectively.

Easy Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app using our in-app reporting tools.

Priority Review

Reports related to child safety are reviewed with the highest priority by our dedicated safety team.

Swift Action

Verified violations result in immediate action, including content removal and account suspension.

Legal Escalation

Serious violations are escalated to appropriate legal authorities and law enforcement agencies when required.

How to Report

  • Use the "Report" button available on all content within the app
  • Select "Child Safety Concern" as the reason for reporting
  • Provide as much detail as possible to help our team investigate
  • All reports are handled confidentially and anonymously

Our Moderation Process

AMIGLY employs a multi-layered approach to content moderation and child safety:

  • Automated Detection: We use advanced technology to detect and prevent inappropriate content before it reaches users.
  • Human Review: Our trained moderation team reviews flagged content and reports.
  • Proactive Monitoring: We continuously monitor the platform for potential safety concerns.
  • Regular Audits: Our safety policies and procedures are regularly reviewed and updated.

Legal Compliance

AMIGLY is fully committed to complying with all applicable child protection laws and regulations, including but not limited to:

  • The Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012 (India)
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 and related rules (India)
  • International child safety standards and best practices
  • All local, state, and national child protection legislation

Law Enforcement Cooperation

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and national authorities where necessary. This includes:

  • Preserving evidence related to child safety violations
  • Providing information to authorized law enforcement upon valid legal request
  • Reporting confirmed instances of child exploitation to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and appropriate Indian authorities
  • Participating in industry-wide initiatives to combat child exploitation

Prevention & Education

We believe in proactive measures to prevent child safety issues:

  • Clear community guidelines accessible to all users
  • Age-appropriate content controls and restrictions
  • Regular safety reminders and educational content
  • Parental guidance resources and controls

Continuous Improvement

AMIGLY is committed to continuously improving our child safety measures. We regularly:

  • Update our detection and prevention technologies
  • Train our moderation team on the latest safety protocols
  • Review and enhance our policies based on emerging threats
  • Engage with child safety experts and organizations
